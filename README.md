Tasthy Application is my submission of Apple WWDC2021 Swift Challenge. It's about application can help you to cooking Thai foods in step by step in COVID-19 situation. Many people in the world are quarantine in home. So I change my mind that why people think healthy is the one of necessary for life. By the way, foods is the more one of necessary for life. I think application that help with teach you to cooking is useful. And I want to tell many people that the Thai foods is delicious that they have to try.

## Technology used

- SwiftUI for design frame and application.
- ImageIO for add GIF to show animation with step.
- CoreData to storage and create Local Model Data.
